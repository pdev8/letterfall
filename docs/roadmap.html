Design reference

Scoring letter values · multipliers · Encore

Rewards hard letters, long words, word economy, stock discipline — and the closer.

letters 1: aeioulnstr 2: dg 3: bcmp 4: fhvwy 5: k 8: jx 10: qz wordScore = round( Σ letterValue × lengthMult ) lengthMult 3:1.00 4:1.25 5:1.60 6:2.00 7:2.50 8:3.20 Encore the FINAL word of a winning deal scores ×2 dealScore = round( base × wordEconomy × stockEconomy × difficulty ) wordEconomy ≤4:1.75 5:1.60 6:1.45 7:1.30 8:1.20 9:1.10 10:1.05 11+:1.00 stockEconomy clamp(1.5 − 0.08·reserveUsed − 0.05·parks − 0.08·recycles, 1.0, 1.5) difficulty Casual ×1.00 · Standard ×1.25 · Expert ×1.60

Only wins bank points. Total points = Σ banked deal scores; daily total = Σ the day's 5 games.

Players never see this math. In-game it's named bonus chips — "Word Economy +30%", "Stock Discipline +21%", "PURIST ★", "ENCORE ×2" — tallied on the win screen; the rulebook explains them in plain language, with exact tables behind a fine-print link.

The Toolkit daily-use tools — post-launch fun

A slide-up panel of limited tools with daily charges: Grab Bag (swap one visible card, replacement drawn through steering so the deal stays winnable) and Scrap (remove a letter outright), more later. Never available in the daily challenge; a Settings toggle in free play, on by default in Casual. Assisted wins count for stats but stay off the high-score boards.

The Living Deck generation v2 — the core
  • Infinite, low-repeat: seeded deals at play time; no pool, no file to mine.
  • Solvable in a par band: a win and a good score always reachable; deal-luck variance kept narrow.
  • The undrawn stock doesn't exist yet: next card = f(seed, move history) at draw time — kills letter floods and lookahead tools.
  • Guarantee, don't guide: an internal escape plan keeps deals completable but is never surfaced.
  • Board geometry (sim-proven): shapes ramp flat [4×7] → steep as the daily difficulty lever; heights position-shuffled; rare letters surface early; vowel-lifeline steering; dynamic bays repair endgame breadth.
  • Daily set: 5 shared scored games/day — the only publicly ranked mode; free play is private.
Modes & leaderboards public daily · private free play
  • Daily Challenge (public): Game Center boards — daily total, best word of the week, all-time points.
  • Free play (private): unlimited, feeds personal stats: time played, games, avg time/game, letters constructed, unique words, most-played top 10, bests, streaks.
  • Anti-cheat: client seeds at launch; server-issued seeds + move-log replay validation in M5.
Word ladder the living meta — post-launch

Daily-challenge results advance a ranked tier; higher tiers retire the community's most-played words. The ban list is recomputed each play day from the trailing week's usage and lifts the next day — the meta breathes, never strangles. The generator plays against each tier's effective lexicon, so every deal stays winnable without the banned words.
